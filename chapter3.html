Chapter 3: A Day in the Mountains

I went to the mountains for a relaxing picnic. The moment we arrived, the cool breeze touched my face, and the fresh mountain air felt refreshing. Everywhere I looked, I could see tall trees, green hills, and clouds touching the mountain peaks.

We found a beautiful spot near a small clear stream. The water was freezing but refreshing when I dipped my hands in it. We spread our picnic mat and enjoyed sandwiches while listening to the sound of flowing water.

After lunch, we walked along a short hiking trail. The path was full of stones, flowers, and chirping birds. The higher we climbed, the better the view became. It felt like we were standing in the middle of nature's painting.

By evening, the orange and pink colors of the sunset covered the sky. Watching the sun go down behind the mountains was the most peaceful moment of the entire trip. It was a day I will always remember.
