🌊 Chapter 1: A Trip to the Beach

I went to the beach with my family on a sunny morning. The sound of the waves made me feel calm and relaxed. The sand was soft, and I enjoyed walking on it barefoot. I collected different shapes of seashells—round, spiral, and shiny ones.

My brother and I built a small sandcastle near the water. When the waves came close, our castle slowly washed away, and we laughed a lot. We also played games like throwing a ball and running near the shore.

Before leaving, we watched the blue ocean for a few minutes. The view was beautiful and peaceful.

👉 Feeling: Happy, relaxed, and free
👉 Best moment: Finding a perfect white seashell
