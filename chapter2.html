Chapter 2: Visiting the Zoo

I visited the zoo with my friends during our school picnic. We were very excited to see animals that we usually see only on TV or in pictures.

First, we saw the lions resting under a big tree. Their roar was loud and powerful! Then we visited the elephants. One playful elephant sprayed water with its trunk, and everyone laughed.

My favorite animal was the giraffe. It was tall, gentle, and eating leaves from a tall tree easily. We also saw colorful birds and monkeys jumping from branch to branch happily.

We learned how zoo staff take care of the animals by feeding them and treating sick ones properly.

👉 Feeling: Curious and excited
👉 Best moment: The giraffe bending down to look at us closely
